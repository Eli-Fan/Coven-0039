Schiaparelli Couture 2026: A Digital Immersive Experience

By Eli F

Couture week doesn't just show clothes: it shows power. This season, the pressure felt sharper because Dior and Chanel are entering new eras with new creative leadership, which changes what people expect couture to "be." In that atmosphere, Schiaparelli's show reads like a response: surreal, sculptural, and theatrical, but also controlled enough to feel like a statement instead of chaos. This digital web lab page uses three different tools to create an immersive experience of the show for those who can't sit by the runway: a Juxtapose slider to compare Schiaparelli's designs to Dior's and Chanel's, a SceneVR 360 embed to immerse the reader in the runway environment, and SoundCite audio clips to let the reader hear the runway music while reading.
